OUR JOURNEY

Carving Legacy Since 1985

What began as a small workshop in the heart of Udaipur has blossomed into a renowned name in wooden door craftsmanship. Founded by Master Craftsman Arvind Sharma, TimberCraft began with a simple philosophy: every door should be a masterpiece.

Today, under the guidance of Arvind's son, Rajesh Sharma, we continue to blend traditional woodworking techniques with contemporary designs, creating doors that are not just functional elements but works of art that enhance architectural beauty.

1985 - The Beginning

Arvind Sharma opens a small workshop with two apprentices

2000 - Expansion

Moves to larger facility, adds 5 master craftsmen to the team

2015 - Modernization

Introduces CNC technology while preserving handcrafted techniques
